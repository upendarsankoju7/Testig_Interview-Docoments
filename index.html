WebDriver in Selenium 4 is fully W3C compliant! All the browsers and the browser drivers in Selenium architecture follow W3C .

Selenium 4 WebDriver was made W3C compliant to make the communication easy and direct between the client libraries and the browser drivers. Improved communication led to more stability.

W3C stands for the World Wide Web Consortium, an international community that develops and maintains standards and guidelines for the World Wide Web. The main aim of the W3C is to ensure the long-term growth and interoperability of the Web.
It creates open standards and specifications that promote compatibility and consistency across various web technologies and platforms. And when we say Selenium 4 is W3C compliant it states that Selenium adheres to the standards and specifications laid by the W3C for web automation.






Our project we are following Agile Scrum methodology. Because of frequent requirement and design changes So, we have to get ready with test scenarios and test cases within short span of time. Because of short span of time window limit, there might be a chance for few of the functional coverages will be missed.


Sometimes the requirement documents are not clear, and it could be one line or two lines statements .In such cases, we have to more closely work with BA and design team to get more information about the new user stories testing. And we also need to closely work with BA & Design Team to Close all query log tracker issues.


Because of Agile methodology sometimes there could be requirement changed completely and also short span of Sprint timelines, we have to test the system on fly. sometimes we have to go with the exploratory testing technique. So, quality of the testing will be impacted in each phase of requirement analysis, test scenario preparation, test execution and test closure phases.


Some of the defects which cannot be accepted by any of the teams. So, we have to take initiative and we have to drive those defects by closely working with B A-Team design team. Development team and support team.


Sometimes developer will not agree the defects and he can reject the defects. We have to set up with meeting with the concern teams. And we have to sort out the gaps. And we have to follow up the defect until it comes for closure.


Sometimes application performance or environments are very slow. So we cannot be able to complete test execution on time. So, in those cases, we have to think about alternative environment deployments.
